Brain Cancer Detection Project

Brain cancer is a serious health concern worldwide. Our project focuses on the detection of brain tumors, specifically glioma, meningioma, pituitary, and cases with no tumor.

For our training dataset, we have considered the pituitary and no tumor datasets. This choice is based on the prevalence of pituitary tumors in our local region, such as India.
